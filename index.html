Sparrow

The sparrow is a small bird known for its adaptability and presence in urban areas. They are social birds and often found in flocks.

  • Scientific Name: Passeridae
  • Average Lifespan: 3-5 years
  • Diet: Seeds, insects, and small fruits

Robin

The robin is a small bird known for its red breast and melodious song. They are commonly found in gardens and woodlands.

  • Scientific Name: Turdus migratorius
  • Average Lifespan: 2 years
  • Diet: Insects, fruits, and berries

Blackbird

The blackbird is a medium-sized bird known for its black plumage and melodious song. They are found in woodlands and gardens.

  • Scientific Name: Turdus merula
  • Average Lifespan: 2-3 years
  • Diet: Insects, earthworms, fruits, and berries

Bluejay

The bluejay is a medium-sized bird known for its vibrant blue color and distinctive crest. They are found in forests and suburban areas.

  • Scientific Name: Cyanocitta cristata
  • Average Lifespan: 7 years
  • Diet: Seeds, nuts, insects, and fruits

Canary

The canary is a small bird known for its melodious song and vibrant yellow color. They are popular as pets and are native to the Canary Islands.

  • Scientific Name: Serinus canaria
  • Average Lifespan: 10 years
  • Diet: Seeds, fruits, and vegetables

Eagle

The eagle is a large bird of prey known for its powerful flight and keen eyesight. They are found in various habitats, including mountains and forests.

  • Scientific Name: Accipitridae
  • Average Lifespan: 20-30 years
  • Diet: Small mammals, birds, and fish

Finch

The finch is a small bird known for its colorful plumage and cheerful song. They are found in various habitats, including forests and grasslands.

  • Scientific Name: Fringillidae
  • Average Lifespan: 2-5 years
  • Diet: Seeds, fruits, and insects

Goose

The goose is a large waterbird known for its honking call and migratory behavior. They are found in both freshwater and saltwater habitats.

  • Scientific Name: Anserinae
  • Average Lifespan: 10-24 years
  • Diet: Grass, aquatic plants, and grains

Hawk

The hawk is a medium-sized bird of prey known for its sharp vision and powerful flight. They are found in various habitats, including forests and open fields.

  • Scientific Name: Accipitridae
  • Average Lifespan: 10-20 years
  • Diet: Small mammals, birds, and reptiles

Hummingbird

The hummingbird is a small bird known for its rapid wing beats and ability to hover in mid-air. They are found in the Americas.

  • Scientific Name: Trochilidae
  • Average Lifespan: 3-5 years
  • Diet: Nectar, insects, and pollen

Pigeon

The pigeon is a medium-sized bird known for its ability to navigate and find its way back home over long distances. They are found in urban areas worldwide.

  • Scientific Name: Columba livia
  • Average Lifespan: 3-5 years
  • Diet: Seeds, grains, and fruits

Swan

The swan is a large waterbird known for its graceful appearance and long neck. They are found in both freshwater and saltwater habitats.

  • Scientific Name: Cygnus
  • Average Lifespan: 10-20 years
  • Diet: Aquatic plants, algae, and small invertebrates
